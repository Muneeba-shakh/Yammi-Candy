About Yammi Candy

At Yammi Candy Bakery, we believe every dessert should be a moment of joy. From handcrafted cupcakes to rich layered cakes, we use only the finest ingredients to bring our recipes to life.

Founded in 2025 by passionate bakers Muneeba and Manahil, our bakery has become a local favorite for treats that delight both the eyes and the taste buds. Whether it's a family gathering or a special celebration, Yammi Candy adds that perfect touch of sweetness to your moments.

Our dedicated team of pastry chefs combines traditional techniques with innovative flavors to create unforgettable desserts that keep our customers coming back for more.

Our Mission

At Yammi Candy, our mission is to create unforgettable sweet experiences through artisanal baking techniques, premium ingredients, and heartfelt service. We're committed to spreading joy one dessert at a time while maintaining sustainable practices and supporting our local community.

Every treat we create tells a story of passion, craftsmanship, and the pure joy of sharing something delicious with those you love.

Our Values

Quality

We source only the finest ingredients and never compromise on quality, ensuring every bite is perfection.

Sustainability

We're committed to eco-friendly practices, from sourcing local ingredients to using sustainable packaging.

Community

We believe in giving back and supporting our local community through various initiatives and partnerships.

Innovation

We continuously experiment with new flavors and techniques while honoring traditional baking methods.
