Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend placing orders at least 48 hours in advance for standard items. For custom cakes and large orders, please allow 5-7 days notice. For weddings and large events, we suggest contacting us 3-4 weeks in advance.

Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?

Yes! We have a variety of gluten-free and vegan options available. Please let us know about any dietary restrictions when placing your order, and we'll be happy to accommodate your needs.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that plans change. Orders can be cancelled up to 24 hours in advance for a full refund. For custom orders, we require 48 hours notice for cancellation. Unfortunately, we cannot refund orders cancelled with less than 24 hours notice.

Do you deliver?

Yes, we offer delivery within a 15-mile radius for a small fee. Delivery is available Tuesday through Saturday. For orders outside our delivery area, pickup is always available at our bakery.

Can I customize my order?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom orders. Whether it's a specific flavor combination, dietary requirement, or design, we'd love to create something special for you. Please contact us to discuss your custom order ideas.
